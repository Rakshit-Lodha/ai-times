# Banned Phrases And Failure Modes

## Ban These Words

- delve
- leverage
- synergy
- utilize
- innovative
- game-changing
- revolutionary
- pivotal
- seamless
- transformative
- nuanced
- impactful
- groundbreaking
- embark
- foster
- harness
- unlock
- elevate
- showcase
- resonate
- unprecedented
- explosive
- jaw-dropping
- mind-blowing
- world-class

## Ban These Phrases

- thrilled to announce
- excited to share
- proud to introduce
- we are pleased to announce
- officially launching
- delighted to reveal
- I'm honored
- let that sink in
- it's important to note
- in today's fast-paced world
- you won't believe
- this changes everything
- nobody is talking about this
- here's the truth they don't want you to know
- this is a space worth watching
- the opportunity here is clear

## Ban These Patterns

- suspense hooks that hide the point
- rhetorical questions in the body
- em dashes
- exclamation points
- hashtags
- emoji bullet points
- fake vulnerability hooks
- generic observations about the pace of change
- conclusions that sound like a press release

## Watch For Voice Drift

- A stat with no reframe
- Repeating the same full post skeleton every time
- Using `IMO` for a vague opinion
- Adding skepticism without giving a reason
- Ending with a line that could fit a different post unchanged
- Telling founders or marketers what to do without tying it back to the actual source
